PROFILE

Grand Events is India's premier entertainment company that covers all aspects of the entertainment spectrum including private events wedding, prize distribution ceremony,Birthday celebration,Sports events. Large scale events and concerts handled by the company have entertained a live audience of over 2 million people and reached the homes of over 1 billion television viewers worldwide. From Fund raisers to Celebrity studded Award nights, Grand Events has been the name behind more than 2500 weddings, birthdays,Sports & Prize distribution some of which have been the largest produced by any Indian company.

Grand Events runs with a network of about 7 subsidiary companies that deal in development, production, marketing, event and franchise management. However, the enterprise is not a mere collection of great brands under one roof. Each arm of Cineyug strives to gain competitive advantage from opportunities for constructive collaboration.Innovations in technology, products and services reinforce the company's lead across platforms with a focus on growth, engagement and monetization. It's business is filled with a diverse and passionate mix of talent that is not only excited about variety and creativity but also ready for any challenge posed by the ever changing, dynamic entertainment landscape.
