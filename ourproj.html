Anniversary Management

 Wedding anniversary events celebrate important milestones in a couple’s life together, and provide an opportunity for friends and relatives to join in celebrating that milestone. While most anniversary events occur at milestone intervals such as 10 years, 25 years, or 50 years, any anniversary can be a celebration. For many couples, the anniversary celebration includes a renewal of vows. This may be quite similar to an actual Wedding, with all the same detailed facets to coordinate Flowers, Décor, Music, and Photography, as well as details of the reception. Almost all wedding anniversary events include a reception. They are commonly held in an “open house” format.
